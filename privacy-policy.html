EyeRest - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

EyeRest does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device.

Health Data

EyeRest stores the following health-related data locally on your device only:

  • Break history — date, duration, and whether each eye break was completed or skipped
  • Daily screen time statistics — screen usage patterns used to suggest break intervals

This data is used solely to track your eye break habits and display your personal statistics within the app. It is never uploaded, shared, or transmitted anywhere.

EyeRest does not access Apple Health, Google Fit, or any external health platform.

What We Store Locally

  • Break history (date, duration, whether skipped)
  • Daily screen time statistics
  • App preferences (timer interval, break duration, theme)
  • Purchase status

This data is stored only on your device. It is never sent to any server.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored on your device for as long as the app is installed.

To delete all your data:

  • Uninstall the app — this permanently removes all locally stored data.
  • Alternatively, go to your device Settings → Apps → EyeRest → Clear Data to erase all app data without uninstalling.

No data exists on any server, so there is nothing to request deletion of externally.

No Accounts

No login, signup, or personal information required.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Notifications

EyeRest uses local notifications only. These are scheduled on your device and do not involve any external server.
