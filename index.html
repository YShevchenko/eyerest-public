EyeRest - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

EyeRest does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data stays on your device.

What We Store Locally

  • Break history (date, duration, whether skipped)
  • Daily screen time statistics
  • App preferences (timer interval, break duration, theme)
  • Purchase status

This data is stored only on your device. It is never sent to any server.

No Accounts

No login, signup, or personal information required.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Notifications

EyeRest uses local notifications only. These are scheduled on your device and do not involve any external server.
